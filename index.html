About the Firm

Trusted guidance for life's most important decisions.

At Lopez Padron Law, P.A., we help individuals and families plan for the future, protect their assets, and navigate the probate and trust process with clarity and care.

Meet Patricia Lopez Padron

Founding Attorney

Patricia Lopez Padron

Patricia Lopez Padron earned her law degree from St. Thomas University School of Law, where she completed a certificate in Elder Law. Her practice spans estate planning, probate, and trust matters, with additional experience in family law, including business succession planning.

Patricia understands the real concerns behind every estate, family, and business decision, and she meets clients where they are — with patience, plain answers, and service. She built Lopez Padron Law, P.A. to be a personable, approachable practice where clients feel genuinely heard.
